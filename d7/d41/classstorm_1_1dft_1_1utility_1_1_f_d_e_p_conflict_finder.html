Get a vector of index pairs of FDEPs in the DFT which are conflicting.

Two FDEPs are conflicting if their simultaneous triggering may cause unresolvable non-deterministic behavior.

Parameters
dftThe DFT.
useSMTIf set, an SMT solver is used to refine the conflict set.
timeoutTimeout for each SMT query in seconds, defaults to 10 seconds.
Returns
A vector of pairs of indices. The indices in a pair refer to FDEPs which are conflicting.
