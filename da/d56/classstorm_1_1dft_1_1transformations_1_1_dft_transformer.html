static

Introduce unique BE which is always failed (instead of multiple ones).

The transformation introduces an FDEP from the new unique BE to the previously failed BEs. Note that this operation can therefore introduce non-binary dependencies.

Parameters
dftOriginal DFT.
Returns
DFT with a unique always failed BE.
